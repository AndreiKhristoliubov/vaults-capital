About

Built by analysts, for analysts.

Vaults Capital began with a simple observation: the most thoughtful investment research is no longer being written by the people closest to the businesses.

We started Vaults to write the kind of research we wished we could read — independent, long-horizon, and unafraid of the work.

Principles

How we think about research.

01

Conviction over coverage

We would rather publish ten reports per year that we deeply believe than fifty that we only half-mean. The market is not short of opinions; it is short of considered ones.

02

Show your work

Every report ships with the model, the assumptions, and the explicit risk map. If you cannot replicate our valuation, we have not done our job.

03

Time, not noise

We write for investors with multi-year horizons. Earnings reactions, intra-day moves, and quarterly noise are not our beat — durable change is.

04

Independent, by construction

We do not run client capital. We do not place trades. We do not take corporate access fees. The only thing we sell is the research itself.
