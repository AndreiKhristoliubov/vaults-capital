Why Vaults

Independent research, in plain sight.

What structurally separates analyst-led research from the desk reports you've been reading.

Vaults Capital

  • Independent and fee-based. No banking, trading, or IPO mandates pulling on the thesis.
  • Three named analysts. Bios, track records, and direct contact — not a brand and a phone tree.
  • Coverage ends when conviction does — not when the earnings calendar says it should.
  • Every report ships with the model, the assumptions, and the risk map. We show our work.
  • Depth over breadth. We cover what we know, not what generates seat fees.

Conventional research

  • Research desks tied to banking, trading flow, and capital-markets mandates.
  • Anonymous teams behind a brand. Access tiered by AUM and trading volume.
  • Quarterly rating maintenance driven by earnings cadence, not by thesis change.
  • Summary ratings up front. Models, drivers, and assumptions behind a separate paywall.
  • Coverage universe optimised for client commission revenue, not for conviction.
