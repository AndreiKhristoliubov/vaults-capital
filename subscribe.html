FAQ

Common questions.

Is this investment advice?

No. Our research is published for general informational and educational purposes. It is not personal investment advice and does not consider your individual circumstances, objectives, or risk tolerance. Vaults Capital is not authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority. You should consult an authorised financial adviser before making investment decisions.

How often do you publish?

The free weekly ships every Sunday. Premium subscribers receive an additional one to two full research reports per month, plus quarterly portfolio updates and an annual outlook.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Premium subscriptions can be cancelled at any time and you will retain access through the end of your current billing period. There are no contracts or commitments beyond the month you have paid for.

Do you take corporate-access fees, sponsorships, or referral payments?

No. Our only revenue source is subscriptions. We do not accept corporate access fees, sponsorships, paid placements, or referral payments. If we ever do, we will say so explicitly and prominently.

Do the authors hold positions in securities they cover?

Sometimes. When an author holds a position in a security discussed, this is disclosed at the bottom of the report. We have a written conflict-of-interest policy available on request.

What if my employer needs to clear research subscriptions?

If you are subscribing through a regulated firm, we are happy to provide compliance documentation, sample reports, and an information memorandum. Contact us using the link above.

Can I gift a subscription?

Not yet — but it is on the roadmap. If you want to gift Premium to a friend or colleague before that's live, email us and we'll arrange it manually.
